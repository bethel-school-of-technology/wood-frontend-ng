Crossfit WOOD started from humble beginnings during the pandemic of 2021. Now enjoying the benefits of a 5,000sq. ft. fully equipped gym staffed with qualified trainers and coaches, our focus continues to be on improving the health and fitness our members and community.

At CrossFit WOOD we specialize in CrossFit, functional movement training and military conditioning. We are located in Fountain, CO and are proud to help in improving the lives of the community as well as military personnel from Fort Carson, Schriever and Peterson Air Force Base.
